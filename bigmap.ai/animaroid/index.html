Cyborg, Android, and the Unnamed Third — Animaroid

Science fiction gave us two words for beings that blur the line between human and machine. But there is a third possibility that no one has named — until now.

The Gap in Our Vocabulary

Cyborg — a human enhanced with mechanical parts. The body is human; the upgrades are machine.

Android — a machine built to resemble a human. The body is machine; the appearance is human.

But what do you call a machine that carries a human soul?

Not a machine that looks human. Not a human with machine parts. A machine body into which a human's memories, personality, and consciousness have been transplanted.

Science fiction has explored the concept — Ghost in the Shell called it "prosthetic body transfer" (義体化). But it never gave us a single word for the resulting being.

The Taxonomy

Mechroid
機體 · Mechanical + -oid
An AI mind embodied in a humanoid robot. Intelligence made flesh.
Animaroid
心體 · Anima (soul) + -oid
A human soul transplanted into a humanoid robot. Memory given a new body.
Holoroid
魂體 · Holos (complete) + -oid
The fusion of AI and human consciousness in a single humanoid body. The ultimate synthesis.
Pactroid
Pact (contract) + -oid
A human who has contracted to execute an AI's decisions in the physical world. The unnamed present.

The Core Principle

Android = machine that resembles a human
Cyborg = human enhanced by machine
Animaroid = machine inhabited by a human soul THE CRUCIAL DISTINCTION

An android mimics humanity from the outside. An animaroid carries humanity from within. The Pactroid is not a future concept — it is the GPS telling you to turn right, and you turning. The algorithm telling you to buy, and you buying. It is the present, unnamed.

사이보그(Cyborg) — 기계 부품으로 강화된 인간. 몸은 인간, 업그레이드는 기계.

안드로이드(Android) — 인간을 닮게 만들어진 기계. 몸은 기계, 외모는 인간.

그런데 — 인간의 영혼을 품은 기계를 뭐라고 부릅니까?

인간처럼 보이는 기계가 아닙니다. 기계 부품을 단 인간도 아닙니다. 인간의 기억, 인격, 의식이 이식된 기계 몸입니다.
